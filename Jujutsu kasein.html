JUJUTSU KASEIN


Action, Adventure, Fantasy, Shonen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a popular Japanese anime and manga series created by Gege Akutami. The story follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student with extraordinary physical abilities who gets involved in the world of curses after he swallows a powerful cursed object.


The series delves into intense battles between sorcerers and curses, highlighting unique and strategic combat styles that involve Domain Expansions, Cursed Techniques, and Reverse Cursed Energy. Throughout his journey, Yuji encounters various formidable enemies, including Suguru Geto, an ex-sorcerer who seeks to create a world dominated by cursed spirits, and Mahito, a sadistic curse who can manipulate human souls.
